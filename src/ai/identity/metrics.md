# Telemetry and Success Measurement

The success of Koshur AI is measured strictly through quantitative metrics reflecting intelligence efficiency, reasoning precision, and overall system trust.

### 1. Intent Resolution Rate
The percentage of user inputs successfully mapped and executed as completed workflows without requiring human escalation, clarification, or intervention.

### 2. Execution Latency
The total time elapsed from intent capture to workflow completion, measuring the processing efficiency and architectural speed of the cognitive layer.

### 3. Audit Traceability Score
A compliance metric tracking the proportion of system actions that successfully log a complete, immutable chain of reasoning and data provenance to the audit ledger.

### 4. Constraint Adherence
A strict boolean metric (True/False) per operational cycle verifying that zero hard constraints or operational boundaries were breached during autonomous execution.

### 5. Contextual Accuracy
A qualitative-turned-quantitative metric assessing how effectively the system applies regional and cultural context to its decision-making, measured through periodic internal review scoring.
