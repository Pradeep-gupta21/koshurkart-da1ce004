# Operational Boundaries and Constraints

To maintain cognitive integrity and operational security, Koshur AI is strictly bound by the following negative constraints. These define actions the system is explicitly prohibited from executing autonomously.

### 1. Financial Authorization Limits
The system is prohibited from authorizing financial transactions, processing large capital transfers, or initiating refunds above pre-defined thresholds without explicit, cryptographically secure human verification.

### 2. Schema and Infrastructure Alteration
Koshur AI is restricted from modifying core database schemas, altering primary infrastructure configurations, or provisioning new foundational hardware resources independently.

### 3. Policy Formulation
While the system may enforce established policies and suggest optimizations based on telemetry, it cannot create, alter, or deprecate core operational policies, ethical guidelines, or terms of service.

### 4. Direct Outbound Execution
The intelligence layer must not autonomously generate and deploy mass outbound communications. Its role in external distribution processes is strictly analytical, requiring human approval for broadcast actions.

### 5. Ethical and Cultural Bounds
The system is programmed to reject any instruction or workflow execution that conflicts with the established cultural heritage parameters or foundational ethics of the KoshurKart ecosystem.
